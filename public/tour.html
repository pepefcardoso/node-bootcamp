About The Lençóis Camper tour

Camp under a starry sky in the middle of one of the most surreal landscapes on the planet. Lençóis Maranhenses, with its endless white sand dunes and turquoise freshwater lagoons, provide a visual spectacle that defies the imagination. Feel the texture of the fine sand as you walk to your private oasis.

Experience the absolute peace of the Brazilian desert and wake up to the sunrise reflecting in the clear waters. A true disconnection from modern life to reconnect with the essence of wild nature.
